Trusted AI Charter

Neural Ethics
Framework

Our commitment to humanity starts with ethical intelligence. We ensure that every neural inference is grounded in fairness, transparency, and the absolute protection of human dignity.

01. Algorithmic Fairness

We utilize diverse and balanced datasets to mitigate socio-demographic bias in emotion detection, ensuring equitable results across all human spectrums.

02. Human Oversight

AI is an assistant, not a controller. We maintain human-in-the-loop protocols for all critical security interventions and behavioral auditing sequences.

03. Radical Transparency

Clear visibility into how neural decisions are made. We do not operate inside "black boxes"—our inference pathways are documented and auditable.

04. Anti-Surveillance

EmotionAI strictly prohibits the integration of our models into passive, unconsented surveillance systems. We stand for privacy-first intelligence.
